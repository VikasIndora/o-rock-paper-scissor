Rock-Paper-Scissor
How to play
  1. Enter all the prompts for now. (Yes, you can't see anything happening. Although you may have already done this step to even see this.)
  2. Open console on your browser via keyboard command:
    • For Windows: 'Control + Shift + J'
    • For Mac: 'Command + Option + J'
  3. Refresh the whole page (keeping console window open).
  4. Now enter your choices for the total 5 rounds that you'll be playing back-to-back.
  5. The computer generates its choices randomly and all of it is logged in the console for you to view.
